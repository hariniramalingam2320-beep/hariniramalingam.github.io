About

I am a research-driven data scientist with a Master’s degree in Computer Science, professional experience as an Azure Data Engineer, and hands-on research experience in behavioral and mental health analytics. My work focuses on translating large-scale human behavioral data into interpretable scientific insights.

I have designed end-to-end analytical pipelines, conducted feature-driven behavioral modeling using wearable data, and applied statistical and machine learning methods to study cognition, sleep, and depression.
